Services

The following are some of the services and topics that are offered and tackled.

  • Individual Counselling

    This involves self or personal counselling sessions. With the safe space created just for you.

    This counselling involves talking and sharing with your fellow trained student.The first contact who an easily understand your situation.

  • Career Counselling

    This involves issues about your professional path and necessary guidelines to ensure you achieve them.Giving you access to materials and even individuals successful in profession to be your mentors

  • Life Skills

    Having problems with normal everday acivities such as socializing?Guidance on communication skills,interpersonal skills,decision making and assertiveness.

    Love is beautiful and to help you maintain a healthy realtionship in your relationship and also keep your performance at the optimum in your studies.

    Our families form our backbone and strongest foundation in our lives.Feel welcome to talk to us about how to improve and better your relationship with your siblings,guardians or parents.

    This involves having support members in a group undergoing similar issues and sharing them amongst each other with a counsellor guidance.
